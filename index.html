Interactive BMI calculator for kids

This is an interactive body mass index (BMI) calculator tailored for kids age 24 months through 18 years.

Enter your gender, age, height and weight and the calculator will give you the BMI and the BMI percentile.

This calculator is different from others becaause you can also change the BMI or BMI percentile to a target and the calculator will tell you what you should weigh for the same age and height.
